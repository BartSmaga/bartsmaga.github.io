Privacy Policy

GENERAL INFORMATION

Smaga Bakery Ltd Apps are all the Apps released in the App Store under the Smaga Bakery Ltd company account.

On the most basic level, we value your privacy and make decisions in our development to respect it to the best of our ability.

STORAGE AND SYNC

All our Apps store your data locally on the devices you use them and sync across devices using Apple's iCloud. Specifically, the CloudKit portion of iCloud, storing data in your private iCloud database.

PROTECTION OF CERTAIN PERSONALLY-IDENTIFYING INFORMATION

No Personally-Identifying Information is transmitted to external locations when using Smaga Bakery Ltd Apps. Non-personally identifiable information such as device type and operating system version may be transmitted to our servers. This is used in aggregate to help facilitate development decisions.

HEALTHKIT DATA

If you choose to have Smaga Bakery Ltd Apps integrate with HealthKit, then the application will have access only to the health data types you opt-in to access. The health data read from HealthKit is used for display within the app. It is not collected or disclosed outside of your local installation of the app. Only those data types relevant to the app are accessed.

Smaga Bakery Ltd Apps request permission to write certain data types into your HealthKit data store. In no case is your health data ever transferred to a 3rd party. It is only ever kept within the application container of Smaga Bakery Ltd Apps or transferred to your personal HealthKit data store as per your permission choices.

COOKIES

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. This website identifies and tracks visitors, their usage of the website, and their website access preferences. Visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using websites.

QUESTIONS ABOUT DATA HANDLING

If you have any questions about this privacy policy and how your data is collected, used, and stored when you use Smaga Bakery Ltd Apps, please feel free to email us at support@smagabakery.com with the subject line “Data handling”.
